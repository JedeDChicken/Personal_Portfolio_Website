About JD Castor

Hello! I'm John Danielle T Castor, 19, from Bulacan, Philippines. I'm a computer and programming enthusiast with interests in General Programming, Software Development, Artificial Intelligence, and Embedded Systems. My journey began in high school when my passion for using modern technology in solving problems took off, particularly when we developed an Augmented Reality-based solution for teaching the cell cycle. From here, I decided to pursue a degree in Computer Engineering, where I learned the various and essential technological fields and concepts, and honed my critical thinking and creativity for developing useful and efficient solutions. During my free time, I enjoy exploring new technologies and expanding my skillset, as well as producing music, brewing coffee, and exercising.
